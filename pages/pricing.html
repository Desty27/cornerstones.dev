FAQ

Common questions answered.

Quick answers
What's included in each website package? ▾
The Starter package includes a static Jamstack site with responsive design, basic SEO, analytics, and edge deployment. The Professional package adds a custom domain, CMS integration, professional email, advanced SEO, and 30-day post-launch support. The Overhaul is a custom-scoped engagement for redesigns and migrations.
Are these one-time fees or subscriptions? ▾
Website packages are flat-rate, one-time build fees. Domain renewals are annual (standard registrar rates, no markup). Modular services are also flat-rate per engagement unless otherwise scoped.
Can I upgrade from Starter to Professional later? ▾
Absolutely. We can migrate your Starter site to a custom domain and upgrade all features at any time. We'll only charge the difference and help make the transition seamless.
What currencies do you accept? ▾
We accept payments in USD, EUR, GBP, CHF, CAD, and AUD. Prices on this page automatically adjust to your region. All invoices are issued in your preferred currency.
How long does it take to build and launch? ▾
Starter sites are typically live within 24–48 hours of receiving your content. Professional sites take 3–5 business days including domain configuration, CMS setup, and SEO foundations. Overhaul projects are scoped individually.
Can I combine packages with modular services? ▾
Yes! Most clients pair a website package with one or more modular services (e.g., Professional + Speed Optimization + CMS). Contact us for a bundled quote — we often offer savings for combined engagements.
